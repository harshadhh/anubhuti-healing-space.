How to Book Your Counseling or Therapy Session

From your first message to meaningful healing and our process is designed to be warm, clear, and empowering.

1
Contact Us via WhatsApp

Begin by sending us a message on WhatsApp, sharing what you're seeking and any challenges you're currently facing.

2
Complete Your Registration

Fill out a brief, secure form with your personal details so we can understand your needs.

3
Secure Your Session

To confirm your booking, make the payment for your counseling or therapy session.

4
Receive Confirmation

After your payment, we will review your request and respond within 12–24 hours.

5
Schedule Your Appointment

Once confirmed, we'll arrange a date and time that suits you for your session or workshop.

Meet Our Founders and Psychologists

The Professionals Behind Anubhuti Healing Space

Behind Anubhuti Healing Space is a team of passionate mental health professionals, educators, and facilitators dedicated to creating safe, inclusive, and compassionate spaces for healing, learning, and emotional growth. Our founders envisioned Anubhuti Healing Space as more than just a counselling centre, a nurturing environment where children, adolescents, adults, and families feel heard, supported, and empowered through meaningful therapeutic and educational experiences.

DD
Darshani Darwatkar
Co-founder and Psychologist
M.A. Clinical Psychology Vishwakarma University CBT Humanistic Therapy Transactional Analysis Art and Dance Therapy Narrative Therapy

"I believe therapy is a gentle, safe space where you can explore, heal, and grow at your own pace."

Darshani works with concerns such as stress, anxiety, depression, relationship issues, self-esteem, trauma, and academic or career pressures, supporting children, adolescents, adults (women), and couples. Her approach combines CBT, Humanistic Therapies, Transactional Analysis, Motivational Interviewing, and Art, Dance, and Narrative Therapy, while maintaining a space that is empathetic, non-judgmental, and safe.

She offers counselling sessions, emotional guidance, coping strategies, and workshops, available both online and offline (50 minutes) in English, Hindi, and Marathi.

With experience across diverse settings including children, families, corporates, rehabilitation residents, couples, special needs individuals, older adults, and conducting mental health sessions with the Indian Army, she aims to create a space where you feel heard, supported, and understood.

English Hindi Marathi Online and Offline 50 Minutes
DP
Dhanashri Pravin Patil
Co-founder and Psychologist
M.A. Clinical Psychology Fergusson College B.A. Psychology Savitribai Phule Pune University Client-centred Therapy Evidence-based Practice

"I believe in walking alongside clients in their journey toward healing and growth, with confidentiality, ethical practice, and deep respect for every individual's dignity."

Dhanashri Pravin Patil is a trained clinical psychologist with a strong academic background, holding a Bachelor's degree in Psychology from Savitribai Phule Pune University and a Master's degree in Clinical Psychology from Fergusson College. She is deeply committed to promoting emotional well-being and creates a safe, supportive, and non-judgmental space where individuals can explore their thoughts, emotions, and personal experiences.

She works with a wide range of concerns including depression, anxiety, panic attacks, OCD, childhood trauma, grief, stress, anger management, adjustment difficulties, and body image issues. Her therapeutic approach is client-centred, empathetic, and integrative, focusing on building a strong therapeutic relationship, enhancing self-awareness, and using evidence-based interventions to support meaningful and lasting change.

Dhanashri works with children, adolescents, adults, couples, and students through both online and offline sessions in Marathi, Hindi, and English. With clinical experience at Kamla Nehru Hospital, Pune and as a workshop facilitator at Ahilya Devi School, she brings practical expertise in both individual therapy and group-based interventions. She strongly values confidentiality, ethical practice, and client dignity.

Marathi Hindi English Online and Offline
